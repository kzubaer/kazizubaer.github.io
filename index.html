About Me

I am currently pursuing my M.S. in Chemistry at Ball State University, focusing on supramolecular chemistry and the synthesis of functional organic materials. With hands-on experience in diverse analytical techniques and method development, my research interests lie at the intersection of organic synthesis and supramolecular self-assembly.

Research Interests

  • Supramolecular polymers & macrocycles
  • Design and synthesis of functional monomers
  • Metal–organic frameworks (MOFs)
  • Degradation of persistent organic pollutants (POPs)

Teaching & Professional Experience

Graduate Teaching Assistant at Ball State University – Instructing chemistry labs, proctoring exams, and tutoring students.

Lecturer of Chemistry at Scholars’ School & College – Designed curriculum, led practical lab sessions, and mentored students (2018–2022).

Analytical Chemist at Eskayef Pharmaceuticals Ltd. – Analysis of raw materials and finished products, method development (2017–2018).

Quality Control Officer at Popular Pharmaceuticals Ltd. – Routine analysis and SOP implementation (2016–2017).
